GSoC deadline has been extended until April 7th

Tue 01 April 2008
by Markus A. Kuppe
in eclipse

Because of low numbers of student applications in general, the Google Summer of Code deadline has been extended until April 7th. However Eclipse to date has already received more than 100 student applications which is even more than last year. Surely we mentors have a stack of reading ahead of us. :-)

Students, use this opportunity to join GSoC. You can find our ideas on the wiki.
