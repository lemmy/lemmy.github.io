eclipse committer status granted

a few minutes ago i finally received my login credentials for dev.eclipse.org and the bugzilla system. :) after my nomination in february and a successful vote thereafter, the whole process was delayed until after the eclipsecon due to workload issues in the eclipse legal department. later some discussion arose between me, neil (the dali project lead) and bjorn (technology pmc) if my public track/record justifies committer rights at this early stage of my involvement with the dali project. i was able to convince him that i'm already ready for this though... so here i am, the newest member in the eclipse committer community. :-)
