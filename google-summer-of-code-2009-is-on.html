Google summer of code 2009 is on

Yesterday the GSoC team gave the green light to Google summer of code 2009. Yay :-) In case you don't know what GSoC is all about, you should read the program faq or join us in #eclipse-soc at irc.freenode.net.

To the rest of us, lets not waste time but start to collect ideas and approach prospective students now! I've prepared a 2009er ideas page on the wiki for us to use.
